Welcome to Flappy Bird - The Addictive Classic Game

Experience the excitement of the original Flappy Bird game, now available to play online for free! Guide your bird through challenging pipes, test your reflexes, and compete for the highest score in this beloved arcade classic.

Game Features:

  • Simple one-tap controls - perfect for all ages
  • Challenging gameplay that keeps you coming back
  • No download required - play instantly in your browser
  • Works on all devices - mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Global leaderboard to compete with players worldwide
  • Free to play - no hidden costs or in-app purchases

How to Play:

Playing Flappy Bird is easy to learn but challenging to master:

  1. Click your mouse or tap your screen to make the bird fly
  2. Navigate through pipes without touching them
  3. Each pipe you pass adds one point to your score
  4. Try to beat your personal best and climb the leaderboard
