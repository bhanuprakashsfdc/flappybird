About Flappy Bird

Game History

Flappy Bird is an arcade-style game that was originally released in 2013. The game quickly became a global phenomenon, known for its simple yet challenging gameplay. Our version brings this classic game to your web browser, allowing you to experience the fun and challenge anytime, anywhere.

Features

  • Simple, one-touch gameplay mechanics
  • Addictive scoring system
  • Retro-style graphics
  • Global leaderboard
  • Cross-platform compatibility
  • No download required - play directly in your browser

How to Play

Controls

  • Click your mouse or tap your screen to make the bird flap
  • Press the spacebar (on desktop) to make the bird flap
  • Avoid hitting the pipes and the ground

Scoring

  • Earn one point for each pair of pipes you pass through
  • Try to beat your personal best score
  • Compare your score with other players on the leaderboard

Tips & Tricks

  • Maintain a steady rhythm when tapping
  • Try to keep the bird in the middle of the screen
  • Don't panic when approaching pipes
  • Practice makes perfect - don't give up!
